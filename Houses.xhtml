Hogwarts Houses

House Gryffindor

Named after Godric Gryffindor, well known for courage, bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. The entrance to the common room was on the seventh floor hidden behind a portrait of the Fat Lady. To enter, the correct password must be provided. House colours are like in the symbol: Deep red and gold. The house ghost’s name is Nearly Headless Nick.

House Hufflepuff

Named after Helga Hufflepuff, well known for loyalty, patience, hard work, fair-play, honesty, and tolerance. The common room is located near the kitchens. To enter, one must tap a fake barrel in the rhythm "Helga Hufflepuff." It was the only common room to have a way to keep students from other houses out (by dumping vinegar on them). House colours are: Yellow and black. Their ghost is named Fat Friar.

House Ravenclaw

Named after Rowena Ravenclaw, the home of the ones who value intelligence, wit, cleverness, creativity, and wisdom. Their common room’s location is a high tower, and the interior was decorated with blue and bronze. To enter, one must answer a riddle from an eagle door knocker. House colours are: Blue and bronze. The only house with a female ghost named The Gray Lady.

House Slytherin

Named after Salazar Slytherin, home to those who value ambition, leadership, cunning, determination, and resourcefulness. Common room is located in the dungeons, underneath the Black Lake, and hidden behind a stone wall. To enter, the correct password must be provided. House colours are: Green and silver. Notorius house ghost’s name is The Bloody Baron.
